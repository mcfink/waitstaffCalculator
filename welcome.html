Instructions

Welcome to the Waitstaff Calculator!

Using the Waitstaff Calculator is quick and easy:

  1. Get the bill.
  2. Enter your bill, tax, and tip information from the check into the calculator (under "New Meal" tab)
  3. Verify data is correct.
  4. If everything looks good, click the "Submit" button. Otherwise, press "Cancel" and re-enter your bill.
  5. Click on the "My Earnings" tab to check your earnings for the shift.
  6. At the end of your shift, press the "Reset" button under "My Earnings" to clear your tip summary.
